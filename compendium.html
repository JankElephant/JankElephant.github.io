Gus Chiggins

 Gus Chiggins is an old magical man that turns gold and iridium into doors to another plane, or something. Does he make a new plane every time he makes a door? Does he link to an already existing plane? I think he explained the process way back when, but that knowledge is lost to time.
  He lives in a pillowed basement in Grayport, across the street from The Infinity Dungeon, where he mostly smokes silverweed all day and waits for the Boys to drop in. He previously lived in Ashborn until it was destroyed in the aboleth attack. There he was the boss man, but he didn't pay enough so the townsfolk all ditched or died in their basements by crocodiles. Then he had to manage the town facilities on his own while they fell into disrepair, until the lads came along and fully disassembled the town.

Places

Ashborn

 Ashborn is a small now-abandoned community west of Westvale. It consists of an inn, a guild hall, a carpentry workshop, and a store (general goods?). Several farms can be found in the surrounding area.
  Gus Chiggins was the last occupant of Ashborn before moving to Grayport in the wake of the aboleth attack that destroyed much of the town. Gus seemed to be in charge of town but he didn't pay a livable wage, prompting most members to leave.
 Another occupant was the gnome Tinkle (no relation to the Tinklebottoms), who was found dead and mostly eaten by the pet crocodile he had living in the basement of the workshop, Tinklespet. The workshop may or may not be haunted.
